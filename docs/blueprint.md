# **App Name**: EasyRide

## Core Features:

- Live Station Finder: An interactive map showcasing nearby scooters and active parking hubs in real-time.
- Scan-and-Unlock: Integrated QR code reader to instantly authenticate and initiate a scooter ride.
- Precision Odometer: A dynamic live-updating ride dashboard that displays the distance traveled and cumulative cost at a rate of 5rs per km.
- Smart-Lock Finalization: Mechanism to end a trip at any available station and process the total charge automatically.
- Eco-Narrative Generator: An AI tool that interprets ride data to generate a creative summary of the user's positive environmental impact.
- Predictive Route AI: An AI tool that suggests the most battery-efficient path based on real-world topographical data and traffic flow.
- Urban Travel Insights: A categorized trip history and expenditure log tailored for city commuters.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary brand color is an Electric Azure (#3366FF), selected for its modern, technological depth.
- The background utilizes a Pale Slate Blue (#F4F7FC), providing a light and clean backdrop that minimizes eye strain.
- A High-Contrast Safety Yellow (#FFEB33) is used for the most critical calls-to-action like 'Unlock Ride' to ensure urban visibility.
- A font pairing of 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for striking technical headlines and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for high-legibility body text.
- A streamlined mobile-first layout utilizing a glassmorphic bottom-dock for critical ride controls during use.
- Thick-stroked, duotone icons that use yellow highlights on blue bases to emphasize interaction states.
- Smooth, rhythmic pulse animations on active scooter pins to denote availability on the map.
